CUHK Course Review

Intro

This section includes reviews of some courses I have taken at the Chinese University of Hong Kong (CUHK), where most of them are Computer Science (CSCI) courses (Update: I have been taking more Information Engineering (IERG) courses during my final year study).

This section is inspired by Yu Chao's CUHK Course Review, which is also available in Some Great People. If you are a fellow CUHKer, be sure to read his course reviews! Moreover, if you feel like giving CUHK course reviews, you can give CUtopia a try.
